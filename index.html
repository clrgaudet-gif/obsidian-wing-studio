About

I capture the raw, untamed moments where light battles shadow in the wild. From the silent power of a raven in the mist to the violent grace of eagles clashing over Moncton’s rivers and forests — my camera doesn’t pretty things up. It tells the truth.

This is wildlife and nature photography with edge — dark, honest, and alive.
