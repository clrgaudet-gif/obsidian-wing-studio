Beauty with a blade

Obsidian Wing Studio is a dark fine-art photography studio built for people who do not want soft beige memories and corporate little smiles. This is for the raw ones. The haunted ones. The lovers, the survivors, the collectors, the art directors, the wild brides, the black-sheep families, and anyone who wants their images to feel alive.

Selected Work

Wildlife, portraits, weddings, editorial sessions, celestial skies, river ghosts, ravens, eagles, and whatever else refuses to die quietly.

Services

Fine Art Prints

Limited-edition wildlife, eagle, sky, and landscape pieces for collectors, offices, galleries, and homes with actual taste.

Portrait Sessions

Dark, cinematic portraits for artists, rebels, writers, lovers, and people tired of looking like they were photographed in a dentist’s waiting room.

Editorial & Brand

Moody, high-impact imagery for art directors, creative businesses, musicians, makers, and brands with a pulse.

Alternative Weddings

For couples who want emotion, atmosphere, elegance, and a little thunder instead of pastel nonsense and dead-eyed chair covers.
